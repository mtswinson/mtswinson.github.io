Projects

Software Development

In the Fall of 2019, a team of three individuals and myself developed a data storage and employee management tool: resulting in a streamlined scheduling process for a local hospital. Through a custom-built user interface, hospital personnel were provided with an intuitive application to assign nurses and doctors to incoming patients; while simultaneously updating local patient records. This project not only strengthened my coding abilities and knowledge of backend arrays, it fostered my ambitions to work alongside a cohesive team of like-minded developers

Research

Assisted Academic professionals in the gathering and analysis of information regarding Bitcoin's past, present, and future impact on the global economy. Through the information gathered and database analysis conducted, a research article produced by Valdosta State University's Dr. Jin Wang is set to reach publication in late 2020.

Game Development

With an underlying interest in video game development, my undergraduate capstone project tested my knowledge of creative game design. Using Three.JS, HTML, and JavaScript, a multi-level adventure game was crafted; with updates and patch notes being presented to cohort members on a weekly basis. In addition to the problem-solving skills gleaned, undertaking this assignment improved my ability to communicate complex concepts, meet tight deadlines, implement visual studios, and utilize GitHub repositories.
